# Product Guide

## Initial Concept
Deep SEO Project: A professional-grade SEO analysis tool designed for website owners and content marketers. It provides comprehensive technical audits, advanced content analysis, and competitor benchmarking to ensure strict adherence to professional SEO criteria.

## Target Audience
- **Website Owners:** Seeking actionable advice to improve site performance and visibility without needing deep technical expertise initially.
- **Content Marketers:** Aiming to optimize content structure, keyword usage, and semantic relevance to outperform competitors.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Strict & Professional Standards:** Adheres to a rigorous set of SEO rules and criteria to ensure high-quality optimization.
- **Comprehensive Analysis:** Combines technical health checks with deep content insights and competitor data.
- **Actionable & Interactive:** Moves beyond static reports by providing real-time suggestions and a drill-down dashboard.

## Key Features
- **Comprehensive Technical Audits:**
    - Full site crawling and indexing status checks.
    - Detection of broken links, 404s, and redirect chains.
    - Page speed analysis (Core Web Vitals).
    - Mobile-friendliness and responsive design verification.
    - **Robots.txt and Sitemap.xml validation.**
- **Advanced Content & On-Page Analysis:**
    - **Meta Tag Optimization:** Deep audit of Title tags, Meta Descriptions, and Keywords.
    - **Social Metadata:** Verification of Open Graph (OG) tags and Twitter Cards.
    - **Rich Snippets & Schema:** Validation of structured data (Schema.org) for better SERP visibility.
    - **Content Structure:** Analysis of H1-H6 hierarchy, image Alt text, and internal linking.
    - **Semantic Intelligence:** Keyword density, readability scores, and semantic relevance checks.
- **Competitor Benchmarking:**
    - Content gap analysis.
    - Backlink profile monitoring.
    - SERP position tracking and comparison.
- **User Interface:**
    - **Unified Dashboard:** High-level health scores with the ability to drill down into specific technical violations and opportunities.
    - **Interactive Overlay/Extension:** Real-time SEO suggestions and metrics displayed directly on the webpage being analyzed.

## Success Metrics
- User engagement with the dashboard and interactive tools.
- Improvement in users' website SEO scores over time.
- Accuracy and depth of the generated audits compared to industry standards.
